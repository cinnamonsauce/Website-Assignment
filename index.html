Why Learn About Thisby?

Thisby has been dubbed the best cat in the world (or at least the best cat in Berkeley). Thisby can be rather enigmatic to the layman. We hope this website will help you comprehend the grandeur of this spectacular feline.

Who We Are

We are The Thisby Society--a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving Thisby's legacy for generations to come. Any information you find here is offered freely, but due to popular demand, we now provide Thisby merch and a donation page to keep the site running (and the catnip flowing). Thank you for stopping by, friends!
